Meet Diane

Owner & Sr Travel Creator
dmack@lamacchiajs.com

Most people see travel as an escape, but I see it as an investment in your most precious commodity: time.

I'm Diane — the founder of Travel Creations by Di. My approach to travel planning is unique. For over 30 years, I've served as a professional bookkeeper, managing financial details and tax preparation for a diverse client base. In an industry where 'the details' make or break a vacation, my background in bookkeeping is my greatest asset to you. I don't just book trips; I meticulously manage every dollar of your travel investment, ensuring that every credit, deadline, and logistics detail is handled with professional-grade accuracy.

From the Texas Hill Country to the Rhine River, I specialize in Elevated Escapes. Whether I'm coordinating a complex family adventure at Walt Disney World or hosting a 'Sip & Sail' social group on a luxury cruise, my goal is to provide a seamless, stress-free experience.
