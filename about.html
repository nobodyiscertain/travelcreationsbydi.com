About Us

Get to know the person behind your dream vacation

Meet Diane

Owner & Sr Travel Creator
dmack@lamacchiaje.com

Diane has two passions that led her to this career: she's a lifelong Disney fan and she absolutely loves planning and organizing vacations. Combining those two loves felt like a natural fit, and about 7 years ago she made it official by becoming a travel advisor.

Today, Diane specializes in booking unforgettable vacations across a wide range of destinations and travel styles:

Disney Vacations Universal Orlando Honeymoons Cruises Girl's Getaways Family Adventures Spring Break

What sets Diane apart is how she treats each and every client like family. She doesn't just hand you an itinerary and wish you well — she handles every detail from start to finish, making sure nothing falls through the cracks.

"I want you to enjoy the excitement of your trip without any of the stress. That's my job — and I love every minute of it."

Diane is available before, during, and after your trip. Whether you have a question at 10 p.m. the night before departure or need help with something while you're on vacation, she's just a call or text away. Your experience doesn't end when you board the plane — it ends when you're home safe with a heart full of memories.
