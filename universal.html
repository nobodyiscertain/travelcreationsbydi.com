Universal Destinations & Experiences

Thrilling rides, immersive worlds, and unforgettable adventures await at Universal Orlando Resort

Universal Orlando Resort is a world-class destination where blockbuster movies come to life. With three incredible theme parks, spectacular on-site hotels, and the vibrant Universal CityWalk entertainment district, there is something for every member of the family. Whether you are a thrill seeker chasing the next big coaster or a family looking to step inside the worlds of your favorite films, Universal delivers an experience like no other.

Three Extraordinary Parks

Universal Studios Florida

The original park that started it all. Universal Studios Florida puts you right in the middle of your favorite movies and shows. Experience heart-pounding attractions like Hollywood Rip Ride Rockit, step into the world of Revenge of the Mummy, and explore the immersive streets themed after Hollywood, New York, and San Francisco. With live entertainment, character meet-and-greets, and world-class dining, this park is where the magic of the movies becomes your reality.

Islands of Adventure

Consistently ranked among the best theme parks in the world, Islands of Adventure is home to some of the most innovative rides ever created. Journey through The Wizarding World of Harry Potter -- Hogsmeade, take on the record-breaking VelociCoaster, soar over Hogwarts on Hagrid's Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure, and swing through the skies on The Incredible Hulk Coaster. Each island offers a completely unique environment, from the jungles of Jurassic Park to the comic-book streets of Marvel Super Hero Island.

Epic Universe

The newest and most ambitious park in Universal's history, Epic Universe represents the future of theme park entertainment. This groundbreaking addition to Universal Orlando Resort features entirely new worlds to explore, cutting-edge ride technology, and immersive experiences that push the boundaries of what a theme park can be. With dedicated on-site hotels connected directly to the park and an expanded entertainment district, Epic Universe is set to redefine what it means to visit Universal Orlando.

Must-Do Experiences

These are the moments that make a Universal vacation unforgettable

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter

Spanning two parks and connected by the Hogwarts Express, the Wizarding World is a must-visit for fans of all ages. Explore Diagon Alley, shop for your wand at Ollivanders, sip Butterbeer in Hogsmeade Village, and ride Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey inside the walls of Hogwarts Castle. The level of detail and immersion is truly breathtaking.

VelociCoaster

One of the top-rated roller coasters in the world, VelociCoaster takes you on a high-speed chase alongside Velociraptors through Jurassic Park. With multiple inversions, a 155-foot top hat, and speeds up to 70 mph, it is the ultimate thrill for coaster enthusiasts.

Universal CityWalk

The entertainment never stops at CityWalk. Enjoy live music, world-class restaurants, unique shopping, and a vibrant nightlife scene -- all without a park ticket. It is the perfect way to wind down after an exciting day in the parks.

Seasonal Events

Universal's calendar is packed with can't-miss events. Halloween Horror Nights transforms the parks into terrifying haunted experiences each fall. The holidays bring Grinchmas at Islands of Adventure and a stunning holiday parade. Mardi Gras celebrations light up the spring with concerts, floats, and authentic New Orleans cuisine.
