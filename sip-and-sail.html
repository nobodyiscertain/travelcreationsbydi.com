The Vision

Travel is best served with a side of friendship

The Sip & Sail Social Club was born from a shared passion between long-time friends and travel experts, Diane "Di" (Travel Creations by Di) and Karen Musgrave (Stamp Your Passport with YaYa).

We believe that the best parts of a journey happen during the "in-between" moments — sharing a local vintage at a Rhine River vineyard, laughing over dinner on a Caribbean deck, or exploring a new city with a community of like-minded travelers. Whether you're a couple, a solo explorer, or a group of friends, there is always a seat at our table.
