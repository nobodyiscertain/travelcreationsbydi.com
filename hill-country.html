The Hill Country Insider

Your Local Connection to the Heart of Texas

I don’t just book the Texas Hill Country; I live here. Based in the beautiful Ingram/Kerrville area, just outside of Fredericksburg, I’ve spent years exploring the backroads, tasting the vintages, and discovering the hidden gems that make our region a world-class destination. Whether you are a local looking for a curated “staycation” or a traveler seeking an elevated Texas experience, I provide the insider access you won’t find on a standard search engine.

Curated Hill Country Stays

Forget the cookie-cutter hotels. From luxury riverfront cabins to boutique guesthouses in the heart of Fredericksburg, I hand-select accommodations that offer a true sense of place and premium comfort.

The 290 Wine & Spirits Trail

With dozens of wineries, breweries, and distilleries at our doorstep, the options can be overwhelming. I curate custom “Stays & Sips” itineraries that match your palate—ensuring you spend more time tasting and less time navigating.

The Insider’s Edge

Need a recommendation for the best sunset view in Kerrville? My deep local roots and professional partnerships mean you get the “shortlist” of the very best our Hill Country community has to offer.
