Meet Your Hill Country Insiders

We don't just book the Texas Hill Country-we live and breathe it. This partnership brings together the best of both worlds: Diane, a local resident in the Ingram/Kerrville area with an "insider's map" to the region's hidden gems, and Karen, based in the DFW area, bringing a sophisticated traveler's eye to every itinerary.

Together, we've spent years exploring the backroads, tasting the vintages, and vetting the luxury stays that make our region a world-class destination. We curate these elevated packages because we believe the 290 Wine Trail deserves more than a standard search-engine approach. Whether you're a local seeking a curated "staycation" or a traveler looking for an authentic, high-end Texas experience, you have two advocates ensuring every detail is seamless, sophisticated, and perfectly "Hill Country."
